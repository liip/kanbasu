--- notes: | Widths are percentages represented by fractions. All classes are constructed like `.w-{nominator}/{denominator}`. By default you have halves, thirds, quarters, fifths and sixths. You can easily add more by changing the `$widths-columns` setting. Widths are independent from the grid, you’re free to use them anywhere, with other components such as mosaic, media, etc. Note that only the smallest fractions are generated; for example `.w-2/4` doesn’t exist when both quarters and halves are in the settings, `.w-1/2` take over it. ---
